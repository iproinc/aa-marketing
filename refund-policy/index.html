Refund Policy

We’re confident that you’re going to love what Adtrics has to offer. That’s because we put in a lot of hard work, positive energy, and even love into everything we create for you.

However, should you find that Adtrics is not the right fit for you, you may request for a full refund within 30-days of purchase and if you meet the following two conditions:

  1. You have completed at least 40% of the Adtrics training
  2. You have attended at least at one session of the 8-Week Living Coaching Call with Fred Lam

To request for a refund, simply email us at support@adtrics.com and we will verify if you are qualified for our 30-day refund policy.

How Refunds Work

Once a refund has been processed, we will remove your access to the Adtrics membership site and community access. If you are on a payment plan and you have requested to cancel your future rebills, we will temporarily suspend your account until you decide to continue with your membership again and resume payments.

How to Request a Refund

Simply email your refund request to support@adtrics.com. Please include the words “Refund Request” in the subject line of your email to help us get to it quickly.

IMPORTANT REFUND INFORMATION: It may take up to 7 business days for your refund to be credited back to your account. It would be very helpful to us if you’d also tell us why you’re requesting a refund. This is NOT required, and we won’t use this information to try and “sell” you something else.
